
##### Primitive means -> 
- Basic
- Original 
- Early form
something that comes from our earliest instincts or stages of development. 

So when we say something is Primitive -> We mean something which is not shaped by logic , morality or culture -> it is raw and natural. 

Examples : 
Primitive Humans = Early Humans who lived before civilisation 
Primitive Emotions = An emotion that comes naturally like : anger , fear etc.

##### Primitive Desires
These are our most basic and instinctual wants - the desires we are born with and not learned.
