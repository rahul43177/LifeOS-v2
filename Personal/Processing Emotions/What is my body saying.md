## ðŸŽ¯ Quick Body Scan Questions

When struggling to connect, ask yourself:

1. **Temperature:** Am I hot, cold, or comfortable?
2. **Tension:** Where do I feel tight? Where do I feel loose?
3. **Breathing:** Is it shallow or deep? Fast or slow?
4. **Energy:** Do I feel energized or depleted?
5. **Posture:** Am I open or closed? Expanded or contracted?
6. **Movement:** Do I want to move or be still?
7. **Grounding:** Do I feel present or disconnected?
8. **Overall:** If my body had one word to say, what would it be?
